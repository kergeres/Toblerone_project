Daily recommended amount of chocolate Science has proven dark chocolate can actually be good for your health. The key is portion control, and it must be dark chocolate.

But what is the recommended “dose”? Of course it depends of your body weight and your life style, but 30-60g/ day can improve your heart health thanks to its flavanols, which serve as antioxidants. But it matters what type of dark chocolate taking.

For the heart health benefits, reach for at least 70 percent cacao, which is fairly bitter without the added fat and sugar.
